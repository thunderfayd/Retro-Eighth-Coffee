Welcome to the Eighth

A neighborhood spot with a whole lotta good vibes.

Tucked into the corner of 8th & Hamilton in downtown Manitowoc, Retro Eighth is part coffee bar, part açaí counter, part neighborhood hangout. We pull shots all morning, blend bowls all afternoon, and bake the kind of pastries that disappear before noon.

Bring your laptop. Bring your kids. Bring your running club. We've got the games, the wifi, and a flight of four mini lattes with your name on it.
